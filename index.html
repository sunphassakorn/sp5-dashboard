The S&P Five Strategy

Each year, on the first trading day of January, buy the five largest US companies by market capitalization in equal weight ($200,000 each, $1,000,000 total). Hold them all year. Sell on the last trading day of December and rebalance into the new top five.

Why the top five? Mega-caps drive most of the S&P 500's returns — they account for roughly a quarter of the index's weight, dominate their industries through wide economic moats, and enjoy the survivorship advantage of being market leaders. Equal-weighting them avoids over-concentration in any single name.
